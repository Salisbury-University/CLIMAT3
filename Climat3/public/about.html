About CLIMAT3

CLIMAT3 is a platform dedicated to classifying information and health research literature through machine learning. Our goal is to provide access to a variety of literature categorized by the Bio-Psycho-Social framework, spreading knowledge about ways to improve overall human health.

Through our state-of-the-art web scraper, we collect and categorize articles from select websites. Our machine learning algorithm helps in sorting and organizing the articles based on the Bio-Psycho-Social framework, making it easier for users to navigate and access relevant information.

We believe that by providing access to categorized literature, we contribute to the dissemination of knowledge that can ultimately lead to advancements in human health and well-being.
