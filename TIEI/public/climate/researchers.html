Karl Maier

Department of Psychology, Salisbury University, Salisbury, MD, US

With an interdisciplinary background in cardiovascular behavioral medicine and clinical health psychology, Dr. Maier studies the interface of behavior, physical and mental health, and education with issues of global concern, such as climate change, biodiversity, and pandemic disease. He takes an ecological systems approach and has developed transdisciplinary conceptual frameworks to better understand the bio-physical, behavioral, and social factors that determine human and planetary health.
